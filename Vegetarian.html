Ingredients

2 tins of chopped tomatoes

1 tablespoon of chilli powder, more if you like it HOT

2 tablespoon ground cumin

1 tablespoon ground coriander

1 heaped tablespoon easy garlic

1 chopped onion

1 large chopped carrot

1 large chopped bell pepper

150g of wheat berries

Pinch of salt and pepper

1 tin of black beans, rinsed

1 tin of kidney beans, rinsed

Give it a good mix

Cook on low heat for five hours

Method

    Using two forks, shred the chicken and give it a good stir. Or don't, it's up to you. I find that it blends the sauce together quite nicely

    Eyeball your meal and decide how watery you think it looks. Does it need thickening up? It if does, start adding some of that cornflour and give it another good mix. Remember, you can always add cornflour but you can never remove it after

    Serve with rice that can usually be cooked for three people in a rice cooker in about half an hour
