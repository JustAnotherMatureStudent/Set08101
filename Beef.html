Ingredients

1 kilogram of cubed or stripped stewing beef

50ml of soy sauce

25ml of honey

150ml of water

30g garlic

30g ginger

6 spring onions

Method

Add beef to slow cooker

In a jug, combine soy sauce, honey and water then pour over beef

Add garlic and ginger to slow cooker

Stir beef mixture

Cook on low for 5 hours

Roughly chop 6 spring onions

Serving Suggestions

Beef can be served with rice or noodles - both are an excellent accompaniment

Roughly chopped spring onions and sesame seeds, depending on how much garnish you want on top of your meal, are a delicious addition
