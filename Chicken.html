Ingredients

One red onion - Jar of easy garlic

Chilli powder - One Lime

Ground turmeric - Peanut butter

Ground black pepper - Coconut milk

Chicken stock cube - Cornflour

Consider adding some chopped sweet potato or diced carrot to provide additional nutrition and texture

Method

Dice one red onion and add to the slow cooker

Add two teaspoons of easy garlic or garlic puree to the slow cooker

Add 1 1/2 teaspoons of chilli powder - more if you like your dishes a little hotter

Grate the zest and squeeze the juice of one lime into the slow cooker

Add 1 chicken stock cube to the slow cooker

Add 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric to the slow cooker

Add 2 tablespoons of crunchy or smooth peanut butter to the slow cooker

Add a pinch of black pepperto the slow cooker

Add 1kg of chicken breast or thigh to the slow cooker

Add one can of coconut milk to the slow cooker

Give the mixture a good mix

Cook on low heat for five hours


Serving Suggestions

Using two forks, shred the chicken and give it a good stir after returning to the slow cooker

Now is the time to add some cornflour to thicken up the mixture if required

Serve with rice or steamed vegetables
