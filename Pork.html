Ingredients

2 white onions

20g garlic

1kg of cubed pork fillet

1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika

2 tsp oregano

3 tbsp red wine vinegar

200g passata

1 pork stock cube

3 mixed peppers

Method

Dice onions and add to slow cooker

Add garlic to slow cooker

Add pork to slow cooker

Add paprika to slow cooker

Add oregano to slow cooker

Add red wine vinegar to slow cooker

Add passata to slow cooker

Add stock cube to slow cooker

Dice peppers

Cook on low for 5 hours - add diced peppers with an hour remaining

Serving Suggestion

Serve with any of pasta, rice or gnocchi. Crusty bread and a dollop of sour cream also works well
